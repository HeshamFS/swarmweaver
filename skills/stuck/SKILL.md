# Breaking Out of a Loop

You appear to be stuck in a repetitive pattern. Take a step back and:

1. **Identify the loop**: What action have you been repeating? What error keeps occurring?
2. **Root cause**: Why is the repeated approach failing? What assumption is wrong?
3. **Alternative approaches**: List 3 completely different ways to solve this problem
4. **Choose the best**: Pick the approach that avoids the pattern you've been stuck in
5. **Execute**: Implement the new approach

Do NOT retry the same approach that has been failing.
